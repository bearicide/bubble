Rules

  • Match 3 or more touching bubbles of the same type.
  • Detached bubbles fall and pay bonus points.
  • After 5 misses, a new row drops in. Rude, but motivating.
  • AFD clears its landing spot plus neighbors, then checks for floaters.
  • Controls: mouse/touch aim, Space shoot, ←/→ aim, R restart.
